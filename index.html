About me

I'm currently a student at UC Santa Barbara. I will be graduating in June 2023 with my B.S. in Statistics and Data Science and a certificate in technology management. While I have been especially blessed to attend UCSB and live a block from the beach, I am ready to start my career in Data Science, Software Engineering, Video Game Development or any similar fields.

Very generally, I enjoy figuring out how things work and then making things work. My journey in tech began with Data Science where I began working on personal projects. I learned that my favorite part was actually deploying machine learning models because people could actually use what I created. From here I started working on projects outside Data Science. Most recently, I've been working on making my own video game and mobile app development which have been very rewarding experiences. If you have any ideas or are interested in any of my work feel free to reach out to me by email or by messaging me on any of my social media platforms.

As apart of my Statistics and Data Science Degree, I completed a capstone project with the local sports science company P3. P3 typically uses their large library of biomechanical measurements on professional atheletes to help with injury prevention. The goal of my project with them was to use that same data to model on-court performance for NBA players. I created a random forest model that can be used to evaluate NBA Draft Prospects and how they would perform over there first couple of years in the NBA.

Outside of class and work, I enjoy watching and playing basketball, working out and playing video games. Although it hasn't always been fun, I enjoy rooting on the Sacramento Kings (light the beam!), San Diego Padres and the Los Angeles Chargers.
